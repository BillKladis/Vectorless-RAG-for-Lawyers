## Sample documents

The legal documents in `data/` are real, publicly published standard-form
agreements from **Common Paper**, included here as realistic test material:

- `data/cloud_service_agreement.md` — Common Paper Cloud Service Agreement
- `data/mutual_nda.md` — Common Paper Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement

Common Paper agreements are released under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0
International licence (CC BY 4.0): https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/
They are used here unmodified, for demonstration, with attribution to Common Paper.

These documents are included only to demonstrate the software. CaseLens is an
informational research aid, not legal advice, and using it does not create an
attorney–client relationship.
